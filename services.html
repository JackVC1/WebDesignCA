Our Services

Assignment Break Downs

Enter your brief into our app either by uploading or maunaul entry. Our app will take the most important parts of the brief and break it down and open a project page for your assignment so that you know exactly what you need to do to get top marks.

To-Do Lists

Once you have your project page, you can add items to-do list. Add all the tasks you need to complete for the assignment, set due dates to ensure you complete then in time and reminders if you start to leave things too late.

Track Your Progress

Our automatically updating progress bars keep you on track. We provide two progress bars one for tracking your to-do list, updating as you tick tasks off the list. The second is a due date countdown telling you how long you have left to submission.

Research

Need help starting an assignment? Use our built in detailed search systems to help you find what you need. Search from a wide variety of articles, videos, books and websites and Smart Guide will offer suggestions and advice along the way.

Neurodivergent?

Stay organised by utilising our project pages where you can keep all tasks, dates and advice together and use our simple bookmark feature to save everything you need for each assignment together. Manage your time by adding due dates to tasks and submissions.

Support UN Goal 4: Quality Education

By using our services you help provide a service to millions worldwide who are struggling to receive a proper quality education. Smart Guide hopes that one day, all can get the quality education we in Ireland get and hope we can help deliver that.
