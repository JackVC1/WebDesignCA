We are called SmartGuide but who are we, what do we do and why? Read on to find out all about us and how we help students everyday, everywhere!

Who are we?

We are Smart Guide, a college and study aid application designed by students for students to help them make the most of their time in college. We aim to help students work in an efficient effective manner a target our software massively helps! When you get an assignment and don't know where to start, have a class test coming up and need to practice or just want somewhere tp keep everything together and nicely organised in the one spot, Smart Guide has you covered!

When researching the set of 17 UN Sustainability Goals, we decided we wanted to make something to help tackle the issues of one of them, as students ourselves, we decided to make something that would improve UN Goal 4 - Quality Education. Many students worldwide are unfortunately unable to access quality education and learning materials - we help make that easier. As part of our commitment to making an app to help improve Quality Education - Our app is also very neurodivergent friendly! Read on to find out more.

Why did we make Smart Guide?

Neurodivergence

We pride ourselves at Smart Guide at being a service all students can use, that is why we are very happy to say our app is neurodivergent friendly! During the development of Smart Guide, we heavily researched different types of Neurodivergence and have applied some services to help them. For instance, Smart Guide helps you rto focus on exactly what you need to succeed in an assignment, helps you find the info and rsources you need and also allows you keep everything in the one place nice and organised and easy to keep track of!

We have a number of goals for our app based on both you the user and us the business. First, we want too see evidence of our app helping to tackle some of the issues with UN Goal 4 Quality Education as that is our main reason for making this app. Second, We want to see reviews from users asll over the world telling us about how our app has helped iomprove their studies and grades. On the business side we want to see our app used globally, constantly speading our reach and also will look to add to Smart Guide in the future!

Our Goals

Smart Guide organizes your college life Smart Guide allows you to simplify and organise your college life by focusing only on what you need to succeeed. Enter your assignemnt brief into the app and it will break the brief down into a more manageable set of info which we call our project page - this page will display the marking scheme areas you need to hit to ensure your receive top marks - learning outcomes from the course and also give you an empty to-do list which you can fil to keep track of what you have done and need to do!

Yes you can! Smart Guide contains a detailed searching system which you can use to find exactly what you need for your project. Search for videos, books, articles and more using our filter options. When you find something useful, you can bookmark it and come back to it later. Smart Guide wants to help you stay organised, to help, you can save all bookmarks, searches and content to your project page keeping everything in the one place, nice and organised.

Yes Smart Guide is free to use In the future we may look into creating premium products but standard Smart Guide will always be free!

You can use Smart Guide independantly of your college and their systems - however as long of your college uses one of the main educational service providers, you can link your smart guide to your college - find out more on the Our Services page.
